# Trademark Prep Packet: BOREAS

For the forensic and clinical psychology software product (the Boreas rename). Prepared June 2, 2026,
for handoff to a trademark attorney. This is preparatory research, not legal advice or a clearance opinion.

## Applicant and mark

- Applicant: Foundry SMB LLC (Colorado).
- Mark: BOREAS.
- Format: file the standard-character word mark first (broadest protection, not tied to a logo). Consider a
  separate stylized/logo mark later once the wordmark is secured.
- Filing basis: likely Section 1(b), intent to use, since the product is not yet sold under this name.
  Convert with a Statement of Use after the product launches under the Boreas brand.

## Recommended classes and goods/services wording

Class 9 (downloadable software):
"Downloadable software for managing psychological and forensic evaluation workflows, including case intake,
record organization, clinical documentation, and report drafting."

Class 42 (software as a service):
"Software as a service (SaaS) featuring software for managing psychological and forensic evaluation
workflows; providing temporary use of non-downloadable software for clinical documentation, case
management, and report generation."

Class 44 note: likely NOT required. Class 44 covers medical and clinical services provided to patients.
Foundry provides software, not clinical care, so Class 44 should be omitted unless Foundry begins offering
clinical services directly. Flag for the attorney to confirm.

## Knockout search summary (preliminary, public records)

- BOREAS, Reg. No. 0920810, Class 29 (edible vegetable fats and oils): CANCELLED in 1983. No bar.
- Boreas Technologies Inc. (Quebec): piezoelectric haptic semiconductors; holds a logo mark and the
  mark CapDrive. These goods are electronic components, technically Class 9, but unrelated to clinical
  software and sold to entirely different buyers and channels (consumer electronics, automotive).
  Coexistence is likely; the attorney should run a relatedness analysis within Class 9.
- Boreas Medical Limited (UK): dissolved 2021. No bar.
- No BOREAS marks found in Class 42 (SaaS) or in the clinical or psychological software space.

## Risk read

Favorable. Classes 42 and 44 appear open. The only same-class item is the unrelated Boreas Technologies
semiconductor mark in Class 9. Mitigation: lead with Class 42 (SaaS, which is what the product is) and use
precise "software for psychological and forensic evaluation" wording in Class 9 to distinguish from
electronic components.

## Supporting assets

- Domains to secure: boreasclinical.com (primary), boreashealth.com, boreasworkflow.com.
- Specimen plan: after launch, use screenshots of the live web application and the marketing site showing
  BOREAS used as the product brand.

## Attorney handoff checklist

1. Full clearance search (federal, state, and common law) on BOREAS in Classes 9 and 42.
2. Relatedness analysis versus Boreas Technologies (haptic semiconductors) within Class 9.
3. Confirm Section 1(b) intent-to-use filing; applicant Foundry SMB LLC.
4. Confirm standard-character wordmark first, logo mark later.
5. Refine the goods and services wording above.
6. Advise on whether Class 44 is ever warranted given a software-only business.
