# Boreas Naming Plan

The complete brand architecture for the product currently called Boreas. Prepared June 2, 2026.

## Legal versus brand

The legal contracting entity does not change: it remains Foundry SMB LLC (Colorado). "Boreas" is the product
brand and product-line name, used in marketing, the product UI, and short-form prose. Foundry SMB LLC stays
on contracts, policies, copyright lines, and the trademark application (as applicant).

## Brand architecture at a glance

- Parent company (legal): Foundry SMB LLC
- Product brand: Boreas
- Full brand name (descriptor): Boreas Clinical Workflow
- Flagship platform (replaces "Boreas"): Boreas Workflow
- Modules and feature sets: Boreas Intake, Boreas Records, Boreas Battery, Boreas Gate,
  Boreas Report Writer, Boreas Review, Boreas Ledger, Boreas Discovery
- License tiers: Boreas Solo, Boreas Practice, Boreas Enterprise

## 1. Company and brand name

Brand: Boreas. Full descriptor brand name: Boreas Clinical Workflow.

Rationale: "Clinical Workflow" tested best with business-side buyers (it leads with the time-saving,
record-to-report value), and it is accurate to what the product does. "Boreas," the north wind, supplies a
directing, purposeful force, and ties to the Foundry family without sounding like a clinic. Use "Boreas"
alone once the brand is established; use "Boreas Clinical Workflow" in formal and first-touch contexts.

## 2. Flagship product (replaces Boreas)

Name: Boreas Workflow.

This is the platform itself: the local-first case workspace that carries a forensic or clinical evaluation
through its full pipeline, from intake to a signed, defensible report. It is the direct replacement for the
name "Boreas." Everywhere "Boreas" appears as the product, it becomes "Boreas Workflow" (or simply "Boreas"
in running text).

## 3. Modules and feature sets

Named for the workflow stages and the report assist. The naming respects the core rule: the clinician
diagnoses, the software never does. No module name implies that the software assesses or diagnoses.

- Boreas Intake: case onboarding, record ingestion, and evidence organization (the ingestion stage).
- Boreas Records: the local case file. Collateral documents, interview notes, and source materials in one
  organized, local workspace.
- Boreas Battery: organizes the test battery and the clinician's entered scores. It manages and displays
  records of testing; it does not administer or score tests.
- Boreas Gate: the clinician-signed diagnostic gate. The control point where the clinician makes and signs
  the diagnostic decision. Named to center the clinician's authority, not the software's.
- Boreas Report Writer: the assisted report drafting tool, the hero capability. Drafts from signed clinical
  decisions and the persisted case record. The clinician edits and owns the output.
- Boreas Review: review-stage tooling for checking the record and the draft before finalization.
- Boreas Ledger: the hash-chained audit trail and report finalization. The "record proves it" layer:
  scanned, hashed, locked, and tied to an audit chain.
- Boreas Discovery: the court discovery and export package (roadmap). Assembles the case record for
  production in a single, organized export.

## 4. License tiers

- Boreas Solo: the individual clinician. Local-first, out of BAA scope.
- Boreas Practice: small and mid-size forensic and clinical practices. BAA available on request.
- Boreas Enterprise: larger practices and institutions. BAA available, with the full defensibility and
  administration feature set.

Feature sets map to tiers (for example Boreas Ledger and Boreas Discovery anchor Practice and Enterprise).

## 5. Naming conventions for future modules

- Pattern: "Boreas " plus a plain functional noun (Intake, Records, Review, Ledger).
- Use words that describe what the software does for the clinician: organize, draft, record, review, prove.
- Never use words that imply the software judges: avoid Diagnostics, Assessment, Diagnose, Evaluate, Score,
  as software actions. The clinician performs those; Boreas supports them.
- Keep module names to two words total ("Boreas X") or three for the report tool ("Boreas Report Writer").
- One brand voice across all: instrument and record, not consumer app.

## 6. Taglines and positioning

Primary line (keep, it is already the site's spine): "The clinician diagnoses. The record proves it."

Brand-forward options:
- "Boreas. From intake to signed report."
- "Boreas Clinical Workflow. The forensic evaluation workspace where the record proves it."
- "Run the evaluation. Own the record."

## 7. Domain mapping

- boreasclinical.com: primary marketing site and brand. Owned.
- boreasclinical.net: defensive, redirect to .com. Owned.
- boreasworkflow.com: matches the flagship product name. Recommended to acquire (not yet owned).
- boreashealth.com: optional defensive. The owned .net already covers this role.
- boreasclinical.com: legacy. Redirect to boreasclinical.com once Boreas goes live.

## 8. Guardrail (words to avoid in any Boreas name)

Do not use, in product, module, or marketing names: Diagnostics, Assessment, Diagnose, Evaluator (as a
software role), Psychometric (implies the software measures). These overclaim and contradict the product's
defining promise. Approved vocabulary: Workflow, Intake, Records, Battery, Gate, Report Writer, Review,
Ledger, Discovery, Case, Record, Sign, Prove.
