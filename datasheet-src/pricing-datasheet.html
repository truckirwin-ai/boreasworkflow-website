Terms & Assumptions

How this pricing works

Founder's rate. The $199 / seat / month founder rate is reserved for the first 100 Pro seats. The rate is locked for the life of the subscription and never increases while the subscription remains active. The founder rate is per individual subscriber and is not transferable. Once the first 100 seats are claimed, the offer closes.

Practice seat bands. Practice is priced by seat band at a flat monthly rate: up to 10 seats $2,500, 11 to 15 seats $3,600, 16 to 20 seats $4,500. Choose a seat count self-serve at boreasworkflow.com/download or arrange through sales. Seats can be added at any time; the rate changes only when the seat count crosses into a new band, prorated automatically.

Support. Every tier includes support documentation and the AI support agent. Pro adds email support with a 1-business-day response for workflow-blocking issues. Practice adds priority queueing, live human chat, and a 1-business-hour response for workflow-blocking issues. Enterprise adds a named support contact with a direct line and a 30-minute contractual response target. Support hours are Monday through Friday, 9:00 to 18:00 Mountain Time. Full severity definitions are in the Support Policy and SLA at boreasworkflow.com/policies.

Founder's rate and group practices. Founder's rate seats are individual licenses: separate accounts, separate invoices, no central administration, no shared templates, and no BAA. Group practices handling PHI should license the Practice tier.

Enterprise. $5,000 one-time setup, then $5,000 per month covering the first 20 seats, plus $200 per seat per month beyond 20. Annual or multi-year contract with volume terms negotiated for large fleets.

Pricing currency. All prices in US dollars. International pricing available on request.

Effective period. Pricing effective from issue date and subject to change with sixty days written notice. Existing customers are grandfathered for the term of their current contract.

Forensic capability. Every tier includes the full forensic workflow: signed diagnostic gates, hash-chained audit, report integrity hash, exportable testimony folder. There is no separate forensic-only SKU.

Deployment. Local install across all tiers. The case workspace lives on the clinician's machine. Cloud-managed SaaS variants are not available. Enterprise customers can request MDM-friendly deployment or on-premise fleet management.

Model access. Boreas Workflow uses your own Anthropic, OpenAI, or Azure OpenAI key. Your key, your data path: case content is never proxied through a Boreas-operated server and inference is never marked up.

Compliance. A Business Associate Agreement is available on Practice and Enterprise tiers. Pro customers operate under standard terms with local PHI custody.

Trial conversion. Trial accounts convert to a paid tier at any point in the 10-day window. Trial extension requests are honored for institutional procurement timelines.
