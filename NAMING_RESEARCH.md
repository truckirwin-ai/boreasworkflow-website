# Product Naming Research

Working notes for renaming the product (currently **Boreas**). Audience: clinical and forensic
psychology practices. Master-brand + module architecture chosen:

- **Parent brand:** `<Name> Clinical`
- **Modules / license tiers:** `<Name> Psych`, `<Name> Evals`, `<Name> Tests`

Methodology: `.com` availability checked live against the Verisign RDAP registry (authoritative for
`.com`/`.net`); marketplace/brand conflicts checked via web search. **Neither is legal clearance** —
a formal USPTO trademark-class search (software = Class 9; clinical services = Class 44) is still
required before committing.

---

## FRONT-RUNNERS (both survived domain + conflict scan)

### 1. Vestal Clinical  ⭐ strongest
- **Available:** `vestalclinical.com`, `vestalpsych.com`, `vestaleval.com`
- **Taken:** `vestal.com`, `vestalhealth.com` (holder not yet identified)
- **Meaning fit (excellent):** the Vestals were Rome's sworn keepers of the sacred flame and
  guardians of the state's records/wills — integrity, vigilance, trusted custody of the record.
  Reinforces the product promise ("the clinician diagnoses; the record proves it").
- **Colorado tie:** Vestal Peak (San Juans).
- **Conflicts:** only out-of-class — Vestal Watches (apparel, reportedly defunct ~2020), Vestal
  Corporation (architecture/engineering), Vestal Technology (general tech), Vestal NY (town).
  **No clinical/healthcare software conflict found.** Cleanest result of the search.
- **To do:** identify holder of `vestalhealth.com`; dig into Vestal Technology; formal TM read.

### 2. Selway Clinical
- **Available:** `selwayclinical.com` (sub-brand domains not yet checked)
- **Meaning fit:** the Selway is a wild-and-scenic river / wilderness in Idaho — pristine, clear,
  protected. Smooth to say ("SEL-way").
- **Conflicts:** none found in clinical/health software. Clean.
- **To do:** check `selwaypsych.com` / `selwayevals.com` / `selwayhealth.com`; formal TM read.

### 3. Colter Clinical
- **Available:** `colterclinical.com` (sub-brand domains not yet checked)
- **Meaning fit:** John Colter, the mountain man who was first to see Yellowstone/the Tetons —
  pathfinder, explorer, endurance ("Colter's Run"). Distinctive, credible, easy to say.
- **Conflicts:** none found in clinical/health/software. Clean.
- **To do:** check `colterpsych.com` / `colterevals.com` / `colterhealth.com`; formal TM read.

### 4. Steens Clinical
- **Available:** `steensclinical.com`
- **Meaning fit:** Steens Mountain, Oregon — austere, dramatic fault-block range. Short, ownable.
- **Conflicts:** none found. Clean (slightly plainer than the others).
