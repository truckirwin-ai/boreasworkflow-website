inside the same Cloudflare stack the product and the future Foundry service will run on. Alternatives that
are also fine: Porkbun or Namecheap.

## Steps

1. Log in to (or create) the Cloudflare account owned by Foundry SMB LLC.
2. Go to Registrar, then Register Domains, and search each domain in the set.
3. Add the chosen set to the cart (register the Boreas set in full; add the crestoneclinical.com
   placeholder at minimum to hold the backup).
4. Set the registrant to Foundry SMB LLC, Colorado. Enable WHOIS privacy.
5. Turn on auto-renew. Choose a 2-year term to signal stability and avoid lapse risk.
6. Complete payment.
7. Park every non-primary domain as a 301 redirect to the primary brand domain.
